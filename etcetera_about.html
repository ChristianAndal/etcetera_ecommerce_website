About Et Cetera

Welcome to Et Cetera, where quality meets sustainability. We are a student-run thrift store dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality products while promoting environmental consciousness and entrepreneurial excellence.

Our Story

Et Cetera was founded in mid-2020 at Siena College of Taytay as part of the Junior Achievement (JA) Marketplace program. What began as an entrepreneurial learning experience for ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) students has evolved into a thriving e-commerce platform that connects customers with quality thrift products.

Our journey reflects the spirit of innovation and dedication that drives young entrepreneurs. Through hands-on experience in business operations, marketing, and customer service, our student team has built Et Cetera into a trusted name in online thrift shopping.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality thrift products while fostering sustainable shopping practices and empowering students through real-world business experience.

Our Vision

To become a leading student-run e-commerce platform that inspires sustainable consumption and demonstrates the power of youth entrepreneurship in creating positive change.

What We Do

Quality Products

We curate a diverse selection of pre-loved items, ensuring each product meets our quality standards before reaching our customers.

Sustainability

By promoting thrift shopping, we contribute to reducing waste and supporting a circular economy that benefits both people and the planet.

Education

As part of the JA Marketplace program, we provide students with hands-on experience in running a real business from operations to customer relations.

Community

We build connections within our community by offering affordable shopping options and supporting local initiatives through our business practices.

Our Values

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, ensuring trust in every transaction.

Quality

Every product is carefully inspected to ensure it meets our high standards before being listed.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to improve our services and customer experience.

Responsibility

We are committed to environmental stewardship and social responsibility in all our operations.

Partnership & Program

Et Cetera operates under the Junior Achievement (JA) Marketplace Program, an educational initiative that provides students with practical business experience. Our team consists of dedicated ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) students from Siena College of Taytay, who manage all aspects of the business—from product sourcing and quality control to marketing and customer service.

This program not only teaches us valuable entrepreneurial skills but also allows us to make a positive impact in our community by promoting sustainable shopping practices.
